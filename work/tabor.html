Camp Falcon Rock is more than a location, it’s a movement. Rooted in Romania, is an initiative dedicated to investing in the next generation by creating a space where churches, villages, and individuals can come together in community and encounter Jesus. Through a Spirit-led environment, Camp Falcon Rock fosters discipleship, cultivates a deep appreciation for God’s creation, and nurtures meaningful relationships — all with the goal of equipping people to actively pursue the kingdom of God.

Solution

Tabor is a documentary produced for Roots in Romania reflecting on the Romanian system and communism’s impact on the religious culture of the country. It explores the organization’s strategic place in the nation and the change they are seeing to the spiritual climate and to the reversing trend of Romanian’s leaving the country as migrant workers to support their families from afar.
